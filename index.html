Why Protect Aberdare Forest?

  1. Preserves biodiversity and wildlife
  2. Prevents deforestation and climate change
  3. Maintains a crucial water catchment area
  4. Supports local communities and livelihoods
  5. Enhances eco-tourism and sustainable development

The Threat: Road Construction

A proposed road through Aberdare Forest threatens its delicate ecosystem, leading to habitat destruction and environmental instability.
